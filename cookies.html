1) What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They help websites work properly and can also help measure how the site is used.

2) Cookie categories used

Necessary cookies (always on)

  • Used to store your cookie preferences and ensure basic website operation.

Analytics cookies (optional)

  • Used to understand site usage in aggregated form (e.g., pages viewed, approximate location, device type).
  • Enabled only after consent via the banner or Cookie Settings.

3) Manage your preferences

You can change your cookie preferences at any time from the homepage footer via “Cookie Settings”.
